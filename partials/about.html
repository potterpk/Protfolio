A bit about me

I'm Muhammad Essa, a Cyber Operations student at Dakota State University with a minor in Network Security. Got into CTFs in 2023 and haven't really stopped — placed well at a handful of regional and national events since then.

Outside of competing, I run enrichment programming for the Governor's Cyber Academy — building labs and mini-CTFs for K-12 classrooms across South Dakota. Teaching students is one of the most rewarding things i do.

Main focus areas: binary exploitation, reverse engineering, cryptography, network forensics, and web security. Still a lot to learn, but that's kind of the point.
